Our Purpose

Golden Horizon is a student-led initiative designed to help parents save money by reducing the need to buy personal study supplies for their children. At the same time, it promotes real-world experience by giving students the chance to earn their own money responsibly.

Our Foundations

  • Helping parents save money by reducing the need to buy personal study supplies.
  • Giving students real-world experience to develop financial responsibility.
  • Empowering students to earn their own money through leadership and entrepreneurship.
  • Building teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills among students.
  • Supporting student education and fostering community involvement.

About

Founded in 2025, Benjamin Boonyoo — an academically advanced student — created this business to blend his passion for learning with entrepreneurship. From a young age, Benjamin has pursued excellence in academics while exploring creative ways to solve problems and help others. This project is a reflection of his dedication, curiosity, and vision for building something meaningful at an early age.

How It Works

Students can fill out an order form with their card details (coming soon). Payment is made in cash at school. Cards will be ready within 2 days after ordering. Cancellations are accepted before the printing process starts.
